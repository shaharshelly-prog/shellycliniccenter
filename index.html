About Dr. Shelly

A Physician, Researcher & Innovator

Dr. Shahar Shelly is a leading Israeli neurologist specializing in neuromuscular diseases and neuroimmunology. After completing his medical degree with distinction at the University of Debrecen, he trained in neurology at Sheba Medical Center and pursued a prestigious fellowship at Mayo Clinic (2018–2021), where he co-discovered the antibody responsible for neurochondrin-associated ataxia — now used in routine malignancy screening worldwide.

In 2024 he was appointed Chair of the Neurology Department at Rambam — the largest neuromuscular unit in northern Israel — while continuing his role as Head of Neuroimmunology at the Technion and directing the AI Neurology Laboratory, which integrates machine learning with clinical neuroscience.

Research

AI Neurology Lab & Research Focus

Dr. Shelly directs the AI Neurology Laboratory at the Technion, integrating machine learning, big data analytics, and multimodal data to develop predictive models and clinical decision-support tools for neurological diseases.

1

Biological Markers for Neurological Diseases & Cancer

Identifying novel biomarkers to improve early detection and monitoring of neuromuscular and neuro-oncological conditions.

2

Cancer Immunotherapy & the Nervous System

Investigating the effects of immune checkpoint inhibitors and other immunotherapies on peripheral nerves and muscles.

3

Blood-Brain Barrier: Drugs & Nanoparticles

Studying how pharmacological agents and nanoparticle-based therapies interact with and cross the blood-brain barrier.
